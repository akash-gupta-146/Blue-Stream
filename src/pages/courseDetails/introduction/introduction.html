Basic Life Support Introduction

The Practice is committed to providing an environment which risks of all kinds and promotes the health, safety and wellbeing of all those who enter our premises staff, patients or visitors, we endeavour to ensure that all employees are protected from physical and verbal abuse while they are working.

As a Practice we are aware that violence and/or aggression may form part of a patient illness, in these circumstances, a discussion will be held with the patient and this should form part of their care plan. This information will be then be recorded in the patient's medical record and flagged to ensure that staff are aware. In addition, where it is deemed necessary, appropriate support will be put in place, e.g. the staff member does not see the patient alone.
